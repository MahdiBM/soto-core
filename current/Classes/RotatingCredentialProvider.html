RotatingCredentialProvider

public final class RotatingCredentialProvider : CredentialProvider

The RotatingCredentialProvider shall be of help if you wish to implement your own provider strategy. If your Credential conforms to the ExpiringCredential protocol, the RotatingCredentialProvider checks whether your credential is still valid before every request. If needed the RotatingCrendentialProvider requests a new credential from the provided Client.
